Paris

  Paris,the capital of France,lies on both banks of the Seine.
Paris is small:it has a total area of 105 square kilometres;but it is
wonderful:Paris is a city with love,a great cuisine,and famous architecture.
The cityhas fascinated writers,poets,and painters.Thomas Appleton (1812-84),
a U.S.author,once said,“Good Americans,when they die,go to Paris.”
