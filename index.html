Paris

  Paris,the capital of France,lies on both banks of the Seine. Paris is small:it has a total area of 105 square kilometres;but it is wonderful:Paris is a city with love,a great cuisine,and famous architecture. The cityhas fascinated writers,poets,and painters.Thomas Appleton (1812-84), a U.S.author,once said,“Good Americans,when they die,go to Paris.”

  Known as the City of Light,Paris has been a major tourist destination for centuries.The city is famous not only for the beauty of its architecture and avenues,but also for its museums.
